Some ground rules

  • To get to the next level, simply update the page name (before .html) in the address bar.
  • All page names will be lowercase and without spaces or special characters.
  • You'll need a modern browser with Javascript.
  • Your reddit account needs to be at least 1 day old.
  • We understand wanting to help friends out, but please don't share your answers. We have limited prizes to give away, and want to reward those that complete the puzzles genuinely.

There's three branches: trivia, computer science, and mathematics. All lead to the same destination, but test your skills in different ways. You're encouraged to try them all!
